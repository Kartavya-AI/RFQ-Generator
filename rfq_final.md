# Request for Quotation (RFQ)

## RFQ Title: Supply and Delivery of Ergonomic Office Chairs
**RFQ ID:** TN-RFQ-ERGO-CHAIRS-2025-001
**Issue Date:** October 25, 2025

---

## 1. Introduction and Purpose

TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd., a leading technology firm, is issuing this Request for Quotation (RFQ) to solicit proposals from qualified vendors for the supply and delivery of high-quality ergonomic office chairs for its Bangalore office. The purpose of this RFQ is to identify a vendor capable of providing superior products that meet our specific ergonomic and quality standards, along with reliable delivery services, to enhance the comfort and productivity of our employees.

---

## 2. Item Description and Quantity

TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd. requires the supply and delivery of **500 (Five Hundred)** units of high-quality ergonomic office chairs.

---

## 3. Mandatory Specifications

Proposals must strictly adhere to the following mandatory specifications. Failure to meet any of these requirements may result in disqualification:

*   **Adjustable Armrests:** Chairs must be equipped with fully adjustable armrests to accommodate diverse ergonomic needs and provide optimal user comfort and support.
*   **ISO Certified Materials:** All materials used in the manufacturing of the chairs must be ISO-certified. This ensures adherence to international standards for quality, safety, and environmental compliance.

---

## 4. Delivery Requirements

*   **Delivery Timeline:** All 500 ergonomic office chairs must be delivered within **3 (Three) weeks** from the date of issuing the official Purchase Order (PO).
*   **Delivery Location:**
    TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
    45 Electronic City, Phase II
    Bangalore, Karnataka, 560100
    India

---

## 5. Vendor Qualifications

Prospective vendors must meet the following mandatory qualifications to be considered for this RFQ:

*   **Legal Registration & Experience:** The vendor must be a legally registered company with a minimum of **3 (Three) years of verifiable experience** in supplying ergonomic office furniture to corporate clients.
*   **Capacity to Fulfill Order:** The vendor must demonstrate proven capacity and capability to fulfill an order of 500 units within the specified delivery timeframe.
*   **Documentation:** Vendors must provide comprehensive documentation of product certifications (e.g., ISO certifications for materials) and company credentials (e.g., company registration, experience certificates).

---

## 6. Proposal Submission Instructions

Vendors interested in submitting a proposal must adhere to the following instructions:

*   **Content:** Proposals must include, but are not limited to, the following:
    *   Detailed pricing for 500 units of ergonomic chairs (in Indian Rupees - INR).
    *   Comprehensive technical datasheets for the proposed chair model(s).
    *   Copies of all relevant ISO certifications for materials used.
    *   Detailed warranty information for the chairs.
    *   Confirmation of ability to meet the specified delivery timeline.
*   **Format:** All proposals must be submitted in **PDF format**.
*   **Submission Method:** Proposals must be sent via email to the following address:
    **procurement@technovasolutions.com**
*   **Submission Deadline:** All proposals must be received no later than **November 15, 2025, at 5:00 PM Indian Standard Time (IST)**. Proposals received after this deadline will not be considered.

---

## 7. Evaluation Criteria

Proposals will be evaluated based on a comprehensive assessment of the following criteria:

*   **Pricing (in INR):** Competitiveness and cost-effectiveness of the proposed pricing structure.
*   **Technical Compliance:** Adherence to all mandatory specifications and suitability of the proposed product based on technical datasheets.
*   **Certifications:** Validity and relevance of provided ISO certifications for materials.
*   **Warranty Details:** Comprehensiveness and duration of the product warranty offered.
*   **Delivery Confirmation:** Vendor's ability to commit to and fulfill the specified delivery timeline.
*   **Vendor Qualifications:** Verification of company experience, legal registration, and capacity.

---

## 8. Terms and Conditions

*   **Right to Reject:** TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd. reserves the right to accept or reject any and all proposals, to waive any informalities or irregularities in the proposals received, and to cancel this RFQ in its entirety, or in part, at any time without prior notice and without incurring any liability.
*   **Proprietary Information:** Any information submitted by a vendor in response to this RFQ will be considered confidential and will not be disclosed to third parties outside of TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd. for purposes other than evaluation.
*   **Cost of Proposal:** All costs incurred in the preparation and submission of the proposal will be borne by the vendor. TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd. will not be responsible for any costs incurred by vendors.
*   **No Obligation to Purchase:** This RFQ does not constitute an offer to buy or a commitment to purchase. A purchase order will be issued upon selection of the successful vendor and formal agreement.
*   **Governing Law:** This RFQ and any subsequent agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.
